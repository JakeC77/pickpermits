How It Works

A managed process, start to finish

Most permitting delays are preventable. We front-load the work so your project doesn't pay for it later.

1

Initial Project Analysis

We review your project scope, site conditions, and zoning upfront — identifying potential triggers (public works, land use, etc.) before they can derail your timeline.

2

Pre-Submittal Plan Review

We review plans against current code requirements before submission — catching missing code summaries, incorrect setbacks, and other common rejection triggers.

3

Submission Strategy & Filing

We know what to submit, to which agency, in what order. For phased projects, we sequence submissions to keep momentum moving in the right direction.

4

Active Review Management

We track your permit through review, respond to comments, coordinate between agencies, and keep all internal parties aligned — architects, engineers, contractors — so everyone is moving toward the same submission. You focus on building, not waiting.
